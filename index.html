About

The Browser is Already a Game Engine was a 10 week course taught by Everest Pipkin with assistance from Tee Topor at School for Poetic Computation. Our class collectively addressed and offered alternatives for both game making and website making. Working with the grain of the web, students used HTML, CSS and Javascript to explore various game mechanics, and ultimately designed playful browser based experiences. Diorama - Andrew Boylan

Diorama is a game spun from the philosophics of Laila Smith on authenticity and intimacy. Leaking hydrants bleed childhood memories into adult reality. A phone will ring with a message not meant for you. Diorama was created in conjunction with the release of Laila's second single from her upcoming EP.

https://diorama-2025-04-02.vercel.app

Park Jam - Annie Zhang

a little whimsical musical website experience handmade with HTML, CSS, and Javascript

https://visibleworld.neocities.org/jam/

Rat Revolution - Chassidy David

Rat Revolution is an ode to the creative underbelly of New York City. Travel through subway tunnels and the city streets, with the help of NYC's mascots (rats), to help save the city.

https://chassidydavid.github.io/ratrevolution

Woods Walk - Christina Cuneo

Woods Walk is a game where you simply wander and look for hidden things you might find in the woods, recording in a field journal. All assets were hand-painted watercolors, with the arrangement and position of objects procedurally determined.

https://cchristina.github.io//tbiage/woodswalk/index.htm

HyperText TV - Eva Decker

Hypertext TV is a celebration of small, handmade games and sites. Sites air at a particular day and time each week.

https://hypertext.tv/

Evagotchi - Eva Khoury

Evagotchi is the artist-turned-online pet, an act of malicious compliance in the current online landscape that asks you to be known to survive. The user has three options to try to pry the most information out of the Evagotchi, rewarding and punishing as they choose. All of the data is the artist's real financial or medical information, and the character is a 3D scan of the artist's real body.

https://evagotchi.netlify.app/

Family Reunion - Leandro Waibe

Family Reunion is a browser-based game inspired by the feeling of being stuck at a childhood family dinner, wanting to do anything but be there. Your goal is to make time go faster and speedrun the dinner using imagination, conversation, and exploration skills.

https://waibinin.world/family-reunion/

Dial Tone - Phil Robibero

Dial Tone is a puzzle game that imagines the world wide web as a phonebook. Give a website a ring to find the next number to call.

https://studiobibs.neocities.org
